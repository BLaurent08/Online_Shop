About Us

Charlene was raised in a home where she was constantly surrounded by greenery as both of her parents were botanists. Her parents had a green thumb and a knack for decorating their home with a perfect balance of plants and furniture. As she grew older, she realized she had this same talent. Soon after moving down to Florida with her husband, she had her first child and two years later, her second. As time passed, she took an entry level job for a local office space company to help support her new family. She was able to work her way up to the Regional Manager for the company and was able to decorate the office space with her own personal touch. When her company was bought out by a larger company, she was given more responsibility and her new boss was impressed with her work. Because of these few promotions, she was able to buy a house and and decorate it to her taste. Many of the pictures on this website are from her own home. As well as decorating her house with the money she recieved, she was given the task to hire a professional decorator to decorate the new office space she was put in charge of. After interviewing several decorators, she realized that she could do a better job than any of them and for much less money. Recieving the help of her parents who didn't quite share their green thumb, she was able to revitalize the office space and make it feel much more inviting yet professional than the building had ever been. After this, she realized that she could do this for a living and decided to start her own business.
